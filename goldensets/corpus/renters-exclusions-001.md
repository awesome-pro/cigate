title: Renters Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the renters coverage exclusions — losses your renters insurance policy does not cover.

The renters coverage exclusions include flood and surface water, earthquake damage, bed-bug infestation, intentional acts, and business inventory loss. No benefits are payable under the renters policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the renters coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your renters declarations to confirm which renters coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
