id: life-coverage-limit-001
title: Life Insurance — Death Benefit Coverage Amount
---

This section sets the death benefit coverage amount under your life insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered life loss.

Your death benefit coverage amount is $250,000. This is the most your life policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $62,500 applies to certain categories within the death benefit coverage amount. Amounts above the death benefit coverage amount are your responsibility unless a rider increases the life coverage.
