title: Auto Insurance — Comprehensive Coverage Deductible
---

This section of your auto insurance policy defines the comprehensive coverage deductible. The comprehensive coverage deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket on a covered auto claim before your coverage begins to pay.

Your comprehensive coverage deductible is $2,000 per claim. You are responsible for the first $2,000 of every covered auto loss, and the comprehensive coverage deductible applies separately to each incident.

An annual aggregate cap of $8,000 limits the total comprehensive coverage deductible you can pay across all auto claims in one policy year. Selecting a higher comprehensive coverage deductible lowers your premium; a lower comprehensive coverage deductible raises it.
