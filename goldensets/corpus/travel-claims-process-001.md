title: Travel Insurance — Travel Claims Process
---

This section explains the travel claims reimbursement process for your travel insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a travel claim within 60 days of the incident as part of the travel claims reimbursement process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the travel claims reimbursement process settles covered travel claims within 45 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
