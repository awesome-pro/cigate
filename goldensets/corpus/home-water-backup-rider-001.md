title: Home Insurance — Water Backup Rider
---

This optional water backup sewer rider can be added to your home insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The water backup sewer rider costs $12 per month and adds up to $2,500 of additional benefit to your home policy.

The water backup sewer rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying home coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
