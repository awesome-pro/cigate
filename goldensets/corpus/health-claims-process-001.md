title: Health Insurance — Medical Claims Reimbursement
---

This section explains the medical claims reimbursement process for your health insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a health claim within 90 days of the incident as part of the medical claims reimbursement process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the medical claims reimbursement process settles covered health claims within 45 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
