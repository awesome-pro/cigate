title: Auto Insurance — Collision Coverage Deductible
---

This section of your auto insurance policy defines the collision coverage deductible. The collision coverage deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket on a covered auto claim before your coverage begins to pay.

Your collision coverage deductible is $1,000 per claim. You are responsible for the first $1,000 of every covered auto loss, and the collision coverage deductible applies separately to each incident.

An annual aggregate cap of $3,000 limits the total collision coverage deductible you can pay across all auto claims in one policy year. Selecting a higher collision coverage deductible lowers your premium; a lower collision coverage deductible raises it.
