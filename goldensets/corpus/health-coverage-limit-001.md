title: Health Insurance — Annual Coverage Limit
---

This section sets the annual coverage limit under your health insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered health loss.

Your annual coverage limit is $300,000. This is the most your health policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $60,000 applies to certain categories within the annual coverage limit. Amounts above the annual coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the health coverage.
