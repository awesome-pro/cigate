title: Travel Insurance — Emergency Medical Coverage Limit
---

This section sets the emergency medical coverage limit under your travel insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered travel loss.

Your emergency medical coverage limit is $100,000. This is the most your travel policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $10,000 applies to certain categories within the emergency medical coverage limit. Amounts above the emergency medical coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the travel coverage.
