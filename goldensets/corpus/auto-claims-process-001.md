title: Auto Insurance — Accident Claims Process
---

This section explains the accident claims process for your auto insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a auto claim within 90 days of the incident as part of the accident claims process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the accident claims process settles covered auto claims within 30 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
