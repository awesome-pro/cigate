title: Renters Insurance — Theft and Damage Claims Process
---

This section explains the theft and damage claims process for your renters insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a renters claim within 30 days of the incident as part of the theft and damage claims process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the theft and damage claims process settles covered renters claims within 30 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
