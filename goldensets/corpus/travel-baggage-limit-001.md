title: Travel Insurance — Baggage Loss Coverage Limit
---

This section sets the baggage loss coverage limit under your travel insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered travel loss.

Your baggage loss coverage limit is $500,000. This is the most your travel policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $100,000 applies to certain categories within the baggage loss coverage limit. Amounts above the baggage loss coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the travel coverage.
