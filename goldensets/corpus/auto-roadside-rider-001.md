title: Auto Insurance — Roadside Assistance Rider
---

This optional roadside assistance rider can be added to your auto insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The roadside assistance rider costs $8 per month and adds up to $500 of additional benefit to your auto policy.

The roadside assistance rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying auto coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
