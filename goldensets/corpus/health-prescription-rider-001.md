title: Health Insurance — Prescription Drug Rider
---

This optional prescription drug formulary rider can be added to your health insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The prescription drug formulary rider costs $8 per month and adds up to $500 of additional benefit to your health policy.

The prescription drug formulary rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying health coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
