title: Home Insurance — Property Damage Claims Process
---

This section explains the property damage claims process for your home insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a home claim within 90 days of the incident as part of the property damage claims process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the property damage claims process settles covered home claims within 30 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
