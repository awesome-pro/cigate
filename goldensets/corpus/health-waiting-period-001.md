title: Health Insurance — Pre-existing Condition Waiting Period
---

This section describes the pre-existing condition waiting period under your health insurance policy — the period before certain benefits become payable.

Your pre-existing condition waiting period is 90 days from the policy effective date. Claims arising during the pre-existing condition waiting period are not covered by the health policy.

After the pre-existing condition waiting period has elapsed, full health benefits apply. The pre-existing condition waiting period is waived only where required by law or stated in your declarations.
