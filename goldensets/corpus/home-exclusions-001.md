title: Home Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the home coverage exclusions — losses your home insurance policy does not cover.

The home coverage exclusions include flood and surface water, earth movement and earthquake, gradual mold, neglect and lack of maintenance, and war and nuclear hazard. No benefits are payable under the home policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the home coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your home declarations to confirm which home coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
