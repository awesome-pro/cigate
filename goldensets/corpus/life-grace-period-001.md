title: Life Insurance — Premium Grace Period
---

This section explains the premium grace period for your life insurance policy and how to keep coverage active after a missed payment.

Your premium grace period is 31 days from the premium due date. During the premium grace period, your life coverage remains in force while you bring the account current.

If payment is not made by the end of the premium grace period, the life policy lapses. Reinstatement after the premium grace period requires a $50 fee and may require new evidence of insurability.
