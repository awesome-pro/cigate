title: Life Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the life coverage exclusions — losses your life insurance policy does not cover.

The life coverage exclusions include death by suicide within the first two years, illegal-act fatalities, undisclosed hazardous occupations, act-of-war casualties, and material misrepresentation. No benefits are payable under the life policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the life coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your life declarations to confirm which life coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
