title: Life Insurance — Accidental Death Benefit Rider
---

This optional accidental death benefit rider can be added to your life insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The accidental death benefit rider costs $5 per month and adds up to $1,000 of additional benefit to your life policy.

The accidental death benefit rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying life coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
