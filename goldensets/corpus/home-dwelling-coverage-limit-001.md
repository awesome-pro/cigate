title: Home Insurance — Dwelling Coverage Limit
---

This section sets the dwelling coverage limit under your home insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered home loss.

Your dwelling coverage limit is $300,000. This is the most your home policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $75,000 applies to certain categories within the dwelling coverage limit. Amounts above the dwelling coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the home coverage.
