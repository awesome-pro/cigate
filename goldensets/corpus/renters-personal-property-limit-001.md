title: Renters Insurance — Personal Property Coverage Limit
---

This section sets the personal property coverage limit under your renters insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered renters loss.

Your personal property coverage limit is $500,000. This is the most your renters policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $100,000 applies to certain categories within the personal property coverage limit. Amounts above the personal property coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the renters coverage.
