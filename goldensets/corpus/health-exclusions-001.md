title: Health Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the health coverage exclusions — losses your health insurance policy does not cover.

The health coverage exclusions include cosmetic procedures, experimental treatments, self-inflicted injury, non-prescribed supplements, and elective overseas surgery. No benefits are payable under the health policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the health coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your health declarations to confirm which health coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
