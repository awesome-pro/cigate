title: Renters Insurance — Personal Liability Limits
---

This section describes the personal liability limit on your renters insurance policy, which covers injuries and damage you are legally responsible for.

Your personal liability limit is $50,000 per person and $100,000 per accident for bodily injury, plus $25,000 for property damage under the renters policy.

Claims that exceed the personal liability limit are your personal responsibility. You may raise the personal liability limit on your renters policy at renewal for an additional premium.
