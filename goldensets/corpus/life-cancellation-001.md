title: Life Insurance — Policy Cancellation and Surrender
---

This section explains the policy cancellation and surrender for your life insurance policy, including notice, refunds, and any fees.

To cancel, the policy cancellation and surrender require 10 days' written notice. A $25 cancellation fee applies to your life policy, and any unearned premium is refunded on a pro-rata basis.

The insurer may also invoke the policy cancellation and surrender for non-payment or material misrepresentation, with 10 days' notice on the life policy.
