title: Health Insurance — Office Visit Copay
---

This section explains the office visit copay for your health insurance plan and the cost-sharing that applies to routine care.

Your office visit copay is $15 per office visit. After you meet your deductible, the health plan pays covered charges subject to 10% coinsurance.

The office visit copay applies to in-network providers; out-of-network visits under the health plan may carry a higher office visit copay and additional coinsurance.
