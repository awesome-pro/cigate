title: Home Insurance — Replacement Cost Coverage
---

This section sets the replacement cost coverage limit under your home insurance policy — the maximum amount payable for a covered home loss.

Your replacement cost coverage limit is $25,000. This is the most your home policy will pay for covered losses, after any applicable deductible.

A per-occurrence sub-limit of $2,500 applies to certain categories within the replacement cost coverage limit. Amounts above the replacement cost coverage limit are your responsibility unless a rider increases the home coverage.
