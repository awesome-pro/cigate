title: Travel Insurance — Coverage Waiting Period
---

This section describes the coverage effective waiting period under your travel insurance policy — the period before certain benefits become payable.

Your coverage effective waiting period is 180 days from the policy effective date. Claims arising during the coverage effective waiting period are not covered by the travel policy.

After the coverage effective waiting period has elapsed, full travel benefits apply. The coverage effective waiting period is waived only where required by law or stated in your declarations.
