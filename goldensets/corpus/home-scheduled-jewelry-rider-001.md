title: Home Insurance — Scheduled Jewelry Rider
---

This optional scheduled jewelry valuables rider can be added to your home insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The scheduled jewelry valuables rider costs $8 per month and adds up to $1,000 of additional benefit to your home policy.

The scheduled jewelry valuables rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying home coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
