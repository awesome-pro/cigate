title: Auto Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the auto coverage exclusions — losses your auto insurance policy does not cover.

The auto coverage exclusions include racing or speed contests, intentional damage, normal wear and tear, commercial ride-share use, and mechanical breakdown. No benefits are payable under the auto policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the auto coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your auto declarations to confirm which auto coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
