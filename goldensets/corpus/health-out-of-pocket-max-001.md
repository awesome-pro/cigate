title: Health Insurance — Out-of-Pocket Maximum
---

This section defines the annual out-of-pocket maximum for your health insurance plan — the most you pay in a year before the plan covers everything else.

Your annual out-of-pocket maximum is $3,000 per policy year. Once your covered deductibles, copays, and 30% coinsurance reach the annual out-of-pocket maximum, the health plan pays 100% of further covered costs.

Premiums and non-covered services do not count toward the annual out-of-pocket maximum. The annual out-of-pocket maximum resets at the start of each health policy year.
