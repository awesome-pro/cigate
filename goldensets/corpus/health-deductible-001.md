title: Health Insurance — Annual Deductible
---

This section of your health insurance policy defines the annual medical deductible. The annual medical deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket on a covered health claim before your coverage begins to pay.

Your annual medical deductible is $750 per claim. You are responsible for the first $750 of every covered health loss, and the annual medical deductible applies separately to each incident.

An annual aggregate cap of $3,750 limits the total annual medical deductible you can pay across all health claims in one policy year. Selecting a higher annual medical deductible lowers your premium; a lower annual medical deductible raises it.
