title: Auto Insurance — Gap Insurance Rider
---

This optional gap insurance loan rider can be added to your auto insurance policy to extend protection beyond the base coverage.

The gap insurance loan rider costs $5 per month and adds up to $500 of additional benefit to your auto policy.

The gap insurance loan rider is subject to the same deductible and exclusions as the underlying auto coverage unless your declarations state otherwise.
