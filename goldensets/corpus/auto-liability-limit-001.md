title: Auto Insurance — Bodily Injury Liability Limits
---

This section describes the bodily injury liability limit on your auto insurance policy, which covers injuries and damage you are legally responsible for.

Your bodily injury liability limit is $50,000 per person and $150,000 per accident for bodily injury, plus $50,000 for property damage under the auto policy.

Claims that exceed the bodily injury liability limit are your personal responsibility. You may raise the bodily injury liability limit on your auto policy at renewal for an additional premium.
