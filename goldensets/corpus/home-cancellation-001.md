title: Home Insurance — Policy Cancellation
---

This section explains the policy cancellation terms for your home insurance policy, including notice, refunds, and any fees.

To cancel, the policy cancellation terms require 20 days' written notice. A $50 cancellation fee applies to your home policy, and any unearned premium is refunded on a pro-rata basis.

The insurer may also invoke the policy cancellation terms for non-payment or material misrepresentation, with 20 days' notice on the home policy.
