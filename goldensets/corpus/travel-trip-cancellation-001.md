title: Travel Insurance — Trip Cancellation Coverage
---

This section explains the trip cancellation coverage for your travel insurance policy, including notice, refunds, and any fees.

To cancel, the trip cancellation coverage require 20 days' written notice. A $25 cancellation fee applies to your travel policy, and any unearned premium is refunded on a pro-rata basis.

The insurer may also invoke the trip cancellation coverage for non-payment or material misrepresentation, with 20 days' notice on the travel policy.
