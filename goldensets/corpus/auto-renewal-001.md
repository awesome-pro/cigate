title: Auto Insurance — Policy Renewal Terms
---

This section describes the policy renewal terms for your auto insurance policy and how coverage continues at the end of each term.

Under the policy renewal terms, your auto policy renews automatically for a 12-month term. Any premium change at renewal is limited to 5% unless your risk profile changes.

You will receive 30 days' advance notice before the policy renewal terms take effect, giving you time to adjust or decline the auto renewal.
