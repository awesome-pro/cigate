title: Travel Insurance — Coverage Exclusions
---

This section lists the travel coverage exclusions — losses your travel insurance policy does not cover.

The travel coverage exclusions include pre-existing medical conditions, extreme adventure sports, travel against official advisories, intoxication-related incidents, and lost cash. No benefits are payable under the travel policy for losses caused by these excluded perils.

Some of the travel coverage exclusions can be bought back through an endorsement. Review your travel declarations to confirm which travel coverage exclusions apply to your coverage.
