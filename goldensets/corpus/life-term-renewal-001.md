title: Life Insurance — Term Renewal Conditions
---

This section describes the term renewal conditions for your life insurance policy and how coverage continues at the end of each term.

Under the term renewal conditions, your life policy renews automatically for a 6-month term. Any premium change at renewal is limited to 3% unless your risk profile changes.

You will receive 30 days' advance notice before the term renewal conditions take effect, giving you time to adjust or decline the life renewal.
