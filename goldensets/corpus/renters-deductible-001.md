title: Renters Insurance — Claims Deductible
---

This section of your renters insurance policy defines the renters claims deductible. The renters claims deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket on a covered renters claim before your coverage begins to pay.

Your renters claims deductible is $750 per claim. You are responsible for the first $750 of every covered renters loss, and the renters claims deductible applies separately to each incident.

An annual aggregate cap of $3,000 limits the total renters claims deductible you can pay across all renters claims in one policy year. Selecting a higher renters claims deductible lowers your premium; a lower renters claims deductible raises it.
