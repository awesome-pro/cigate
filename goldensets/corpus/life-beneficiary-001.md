title: Life Insurance — Beneficiary Claim Process
---

This section explains the beneficiary death claim process for your life insurance policy and the documentation required to receive payment.

You must file a life claim within 90 days of the incident as part of the beneficiary death claim process. Provide a completed claim form, supporting receipts, and any police or incident reports.

Once documentation is complete, the beneficiary death claim process settles covered life claims within 30 days. Disputed amounts may be appealed in writing.
